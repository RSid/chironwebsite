About Us

Chiron Sciences' breakthrough, patented approach to producing high-performing ultra-fine iron powder has resulted in a product that is substantially more effective not only as a nutritional fortification product, but also as a tool for environmental remediation and a chemical catalyst than any other product on the market.

We have proven our technology through a series of production runs at our pilot plant and through scientific tests that confirm the effectiveness of our product. We are currently in the final design phase of our first to-scale production facility, and expect to begin commercial deliveries in mid-2014.
